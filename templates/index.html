Welcome to Poke n Hope's official website!

We have several sections now. In Live, you will be able to see the real-time score during matches on Tuesday and Wednesday, if there is someone to take the charge of inputing scores. In Schedule, you will see all the 14-week matches and you are able to click to see results for matches alread completed. In Stats, you will see the normal Player and Team ranking. And the last Archive section allows to you see all matches recorded in the system.

We have to mention in particular on the Player Ranking page a new measure which is commonly used in sport competitions. We encourage you to go here for some documentation. It is a ranking system that takes into account your opponent's level when calculating your ranking points. You will not be over-penalised (over-rewarded) if you play a strong (less skill) player.

The ranking points will not affect the final prize for this season. It only tells you relative level compared to others. If you think you are underrated in this ranking, what you can do? The most efficient way is putting your opponent in the pressure and having more run outs.

We have dedicated a lot of time on launching this website. We would be happy to see it really brings something to you and your team. We are trying our best to adding more features as time goes on. And any suggestions or bugs reporting are more than welcome.

Let's together make a great pool community! You can contact us at poke.n.hope.hk@gmail.com.
