Welcome to Poke n Hope's official website!

We have several sections now. In Live, you will be able to see the real-time score during matches on Tuesday and Wednesday, if there is someone takes the charge of inputing the scores. In Schedule, you will see all the 14-week matches and you are able to click to see results for matches alread completed. In Stats, you will see the normal Player and Team ranking. And the last Archive section allows to you see all matches recorded in the system.

We have to mention in particular on the Player Ranking page a new measure which is commonly used in sport competitions. We encourage you to go here for some documentation. But for those who hate numbers and formulas, we will explain why we have done so in simple words. Let's look at the following situations:
  • Imagine you play a good player and you are at the same level. Your opponent breaks and runs out the table. You get a 0:10. Then you break and run out as well. Both you and your opponent played excellent pool. But in the current total-points/average framework, you both would be penalized for getting 10 points in total in 2 frames.
  • Now imagine you play a player much less skilled than you. You play bad and missed several shots but your opponent plays much better than her expected level. In the end, she still lost to you 7:10 and 7:10. In the current framework, you get 20 in two frames and it is perfect. You don't have to pay for your struggle.
But in the Ranking Points evaluation, it is different. In the first case, your ranking points will not be affected at all; in the second, your ranking points will decrease and also your opponent will be rewarded for pushing you to the edge.
